About

I’m a third year Ph.D. candidate at the School of Data Science, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ) supervised by Prof. Guiliang Liu and co-supervised by Prof. Hongyuan Zha.
Before, I received my Bachelor's degree (rank: 1/225) at the School of Computer Science, Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU) in 2023. I was honored to be selected as the Graduate Representative and recognized as the Provincial Outstanding Graduate.
My research focuses on:
  • Risk-sensitive / Robust Reinforcement Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning Applications (e.g., Embodied AI, Sports Science)
