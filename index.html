About

I’m a second year PhD student at the School of Data Science, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ) supervised by Prof. Guiliang Liu and co-supervised by Prof. Hongyuan Zha.
Before, I received my Bachelor degree (rank: 1/225) at the School of Computer Science, Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU) in 2023.
My research focuses on:
  • Inverse Constrained Reinforcement Learning
  • Risk-sensitive / Distributional Reinforcement Learning
  • Preference-based Reinforcement Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning Applications in Sports Science
